# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Never share OTP, UPI PIN, or passwords with anyone  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown attachments  
- Always verify the identity of callers claiming to be from banks or companies  
- Use official apps downloaded only from trusted sources (Play Store/App Store)  
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible  
- Keep your apps and phone software updated regularly  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Regularly monitor your bank statements for unauthorized transactions  

## UPI / Financial Safety Tips
- Remember: Entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it  
- Never approve collect requests from unknown users  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930 (available 24×7)
