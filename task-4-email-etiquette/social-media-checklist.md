# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s
- Think before posting; ensure content is appropriate and respectful  
- Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication  
- Verify information before sharing  
- Maintain a professional online presence  
- Adjust privacy settings to control who can view your content  

## Don’ts
- Do not share sensitive personal information (OTP, address, passwords)  
- Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful content  
- Do not click on suspicious links or messages  
- Do not overshare private life details publicly  
- Do not engage in online arguments or cyberbullying
